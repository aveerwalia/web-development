My Fitness Routine Cards

Follow this fitness routine consistently every day to get in shape in only one month!

Morning Stretch

Begin the day with simple stretches for your arms, legs, back, and neck.

Duration: 10 minutes

Cardio Workout

Do Star Jumps (jumping jacks), jogging, or skipping to improve stamina and energy.

Duration: 20 minutes

Strength Training

Practise squats, push-ups, and planks to build body strength.

Duration: 15 minutes

Yoga

Try simple yoga poses to improve balance, flexibility, and focus.

Duration: 15 minutes

Evening Walk

Go for a relaxing walk to stay active and refresh your mind.

Duration: 25 minutes

Rest and Hydrate

Drink enough water and take proper rest after completing your workout.

Duration: All day

Optional Activity

Hit the gym at any time of the day to burn some extra calories (remember not to go too hard on yourself).

Duration: 30min-1hr (Optional)

Fitness Tip: A good routine includes exercise, rest, a healthy-full of protein diet, and consistency.
