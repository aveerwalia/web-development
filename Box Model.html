Snow Leopard

Type: Big cat

Habitat: Lives in mountain range of central Asia

Diet: Deer, Goats and wild Sheep

lion

Diet: Being carnivores Lions enjoy feasting on animals like Zebras and Giraffes along with many others.

Cool Fact: Lions are often known as the king of the jungle and very feared but ferocious creatures
